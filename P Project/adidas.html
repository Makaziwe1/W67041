Adidas AG - a German company producing footwear and sportswear, founded in 1924 by the brothers Adolf and Rudolf. The company is divided into three parts: Adidas Sport, Adidas Classic and Adidas Style. The Adidas trademark is the three stripes that are placed on their products, e.g. on the arms of football kits. In addition to shoes and clothing, the company also produces sports equipment, such as gloves and balls
