Nike Sneakers

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing,and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. The company takes its name from Nike, the Greek goddess of victory. Nike markets its products under its own brand, as well as Nike Golf, Nike Pro, Nike+, Air Jordan, Nike Blazers, Air Force 1, Nike Dunk, Air Max, Foamposite, Nike Skateboarding, Nike CR7 and subsidiaries including Air Jordan and Converse.
