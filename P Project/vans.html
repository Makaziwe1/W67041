VANS Sneakers

Vans is an American manufacturer of skateboarding shoes and related apparel, established in Anaheim, California, and owned by VF Corporation. The company also sponsors surf, snowboarding, BMX, and motocross teams. From 1996 to 2019, the company was the primary sponsor of the annual Warped Tour music festival.

Today, the famous Vans side stripe has become the flagship and widely recognized mark of the Vans brand. With sturdy suede and canvas uppers in refreshed colorways, these low-top lace-up Old Skool shoes pay homage to the brand's heritage. It also features re-enforced toecaps, padded collars for added comfort, and signature rubber waffle outsoles.

Today, the Classic Slip-On is known around the world for its comfortable silhouette, ease of entry and beloved design. Featuring the iconic Vans checkerboard design, the Classic Slip-On is the perfect off-the-wall option for everyday wear. It is completed with low profile canvas uppers, padded collars for support, elastic side accents and signature rubber waffle outsoles.

The Sk8-Hi is just the second model to bear the branding previously known as "jazz stripe" that has given Vans footwear a whole new look. Taking inspiration from the first high-top design, the Sk8-Hi features sturdy suede and canvas uppers in classic and unusual colors. It also features re-enforced toecaps, padded collars for added comfort, and signature rubber waffle outsoles.
